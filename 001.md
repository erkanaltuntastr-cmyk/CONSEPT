# Oakwood – First Vertical & MVP Definition (Gym)

## Decision Status

**FIRST VERTICAL SELECTED: GYM / FITNESS**
Status: **LOCKED**

This decision is final for Phase 1. Other ideas are valid but intentionally deferred.

---

## Why Gym First (Business Rationale)

Gym is the correct first vertical because it:

* Is already in active development (sunk effort acknowledged, not ignored)
* Has clear, repeatable behaviour (sessions, routines, frequency)
* Naturally fits the Easy Tracking philosophy
* Provides fast feedback loops (daily / weekly usage)
* Has a clear institutional buyer path (gyms, PTs, chains)

Gym is a **contained system**, not an endless life-domain.
This makes it ideal for proving the Oakwood model.

---

## Target Users (Phase 1)

### Primary User

* Individual gym-goers
* Parents / adults managing their own routines

### Secondary (Future Phase)

* Gyms
* Personal trainers
* Multi-location fitness operators

Phase 1 is **B2C-led**, with B2B enabled by design.

---

## Core Problem to Solve (One Sentence)

> Gym tracking is either too complex to sustain or too shallow to be useful.

Oakwood Gym solves this by:

* Reducing input to seconds
* Preserving meaningful progress signals
* Eliminating unnecessary dashboards

---

## MVP Definition (Non-Negotiable)

The MVP must:

* Be usable in under 10 seconds per session
* Work from a single primary screen
* Require no manual configuration to start
* Produce a clear sense of progress without analytics overload

If a feature violates any of the above, it is cut.

---

## Explicitly Out of Scope (Phase 1)

* Social features
* Leaderboards
* Gamification mechanics
* Coaching marketplaces
* Complex exercise libraries
* Heavy analytics

These are consciously rejected.

---

## Success Metrics (Phase 1)

We measure success by:

1. Continued usage after 30 days
2. User ability to log without thinking
3. Willingness to pay a small annual fee

Not by:

* Time spent in app
* Number of screens
* Feature count

---

## Codex Status

* Active development underway
* Architecture direction accepted
* Code quality reviewed at abstract level

Next step is **product completion**, not expansion.

---

## Rule for Expansion

No second vertical is considered until:

* Gym MVP is stable
* Real users exist
* Behavioural patterns are observed

Ideas are parked, not discarded.
